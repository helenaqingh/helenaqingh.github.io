TV Shows That I Like

《鶴唳華亭》 (Royal Nirvana) is a historical drama based on the novel by Xue Man Liang Yuan, released in 2019. The story follows Lu Wenxi, a Confucian scholar who enters the complex world of the royal court to seek justice for her wrongfully accused father and brother. The series holds deep value for its classical Chinese aesthetics and its exploration of Confucian ideals, feudal order, and the human condition. My favorite line from the series is: 「可得解脱处，唯神佛前，与山水间」 — one finds release only before the divine, or among mountains and rivers.
